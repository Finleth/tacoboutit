Moroccan Lamb\n=============\n\nA Differently Spiced Meat Than Your Usual Taco\n----------------------------------------------/n/n* 500 g ground lamb/n* 20 g Ras al Hanout spice. My mix contains:\n * Paprika\n * Cinnamon\n * Allspice\n * Clove\n * Toasted Mustard\n * Cumin\n * Caraway\n * Black Pepper\n* 10 g fresh lemon zest or 10 g finely ground Black Lemon Omani (Moroccoan dried preserved lemon)\n\nMix together and brown.\n
